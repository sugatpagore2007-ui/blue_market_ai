# Sample Blue Strategy Notes

These are only sample notes to test the Phase 12 importer. Replace them with your own notes from the videos.

- Avoid trading when high impact news is close and spread becomes unstable.
- If the market is sideways and price is choppy, skip the trade even if one indicator gives a signal.
- A liquidity sweep becomes stronger when price returns with displacement and higher timeframe bias agrees.
- Entry confirmation should come after structure shift, retest, or clean imbalance reaction.
- Risk reward must be checked before entry; weak RR is a no trade condition.
- Move stop loss to breakeven only after the planned level is reached, not because of fear.
- Use a trade journal to review mistakes and avoid revenge trading.
