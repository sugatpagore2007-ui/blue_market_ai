## Lessons
- Avoid trading during high-impact news if spread and volatility are unstable.
- If liquidity is swept and price shows displacement back into the range, wait for a clean entry trigger.
- Only take a setup if higher timeframe bias and entry timeframe agree.
- Skip trades in choppy ranges when risk reward is weak.
- Move stop to breakeven only after price reaches planned management level.
